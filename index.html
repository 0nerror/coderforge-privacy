Privacy Policy

Codersforge built this app as a standalone application. This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal data.

All data entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device only. No data is transmitted off the device, accessed by Codersforge, or shared with any third parties.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No analytics or tracking are used
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No data is shared with third parties

Because all data remains on the user’s device, Codersforge has no access to user data under any circumstances.
